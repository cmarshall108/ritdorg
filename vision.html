Vision

RITD recognizes God revealing Himself in the Scriptures and using the nation of Israel both in the Old and New Testaments to demonstrate His grace.

RITD envisions its worldview as a facilitator in servitude to God's purposes, coordination its efforts in charitable, religious and educational contributions to humanity.

Mission

RITD purposes to educate people, from children to adults, about the Hebrew language, traditions, and culture, through media and technology-based presentations, conferences, tours and publications, cooperating with project based ministries and publishers.

RITD uses a multi-lingual, multi-media, multi-location; Bible based approach to recognize the Hebraic and Jewish flavor of both Testaments, the prophetic fulfillment of Israel as a national entity, the Gentile relationship to Jewish Israel, and the continuance of the Gospel through the Messiahship of Jesus.

RITD invites individuals and groups to participate in humanitarian aid to Israel.
